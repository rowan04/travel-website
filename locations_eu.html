SCHLOSS NEUCHWANSTEIN

Neuchwanstein Castle is a 19th-century historicist palace situated on a rugged hill at the foothills of the Alps in the very south of Germany, near the town of Fussen.

The turreted castle is stunning with views that are even better, of mountains, lakes, plains and it's sister castle nearby.

The location is easily accessible, with nearby parking and a gift shop. The castle even does tours of its furnished rooms. It is truly a brilliant place to visit.

Tripadvisor: 4.5/5 Stars

Coordinates: 47.558, 10.750

MONACO

Monaco is a principality and soverign city-state in the very south-east of France. It borders the Mediterranean Sea, and has a population of over 38,000.

Monaco is widely recognised as one of the most expensive and wealthiest places in the world, with plenty of Casinos (such as the famous Monte Carlo), bars, restaurants and shops.

Monaco has a beautiful harbour, its own F1 race and stunning mountains that overlook the city. Add to that the wonderful old architecture, and it makes Monaco one of the best holiday destinations.

Tripadvisor: 5/5 Stars

Coordinates: 43.734, 7.421
